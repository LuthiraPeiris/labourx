# LabourX 🏗️ — Construction Technician Marketplace

**Domain:** E-Commerce & Marketplace Solutions

LabourX is a clean, professional marketplace that connects homeowners and project owners with skilled construction technicians such as masons, electricians, plumbers, interior designers, architects, and other construction-related professionals.

It brings together three powerful ideas in one platform:

* 🔎 **Search & discover** nearby professionals by trade
* 🧰 **Portfolio-first profiles** to judge real quality before hiring
* 📝 **Post jobs and compare proposals** when users want multiple options

LabourX helps users hire with confidence while helping technicians win work through credibility, visibility, and trust. 🤝

## Core Features ✨

### Technician Profiles — Portfolio First 🧰

Technicians can create profiles that communicate value quickly and professionally.

A technician profile can include:

* 🏷️ Trade and specialization
* 🧱 Previous projects and quality of work
* 📚 Experience and education
* 🗓️ Work style, expectations, and availability
* 📞 Contact details for quick communication
* ⭐ Ratings and reviews from past clients
* 🚀 Boost badge or featured status if promoted

This makes it easier for users to judge quality before contacting a technician.

---

### User Profiles 🪪

Users can maintain essential profile details to support communication, job posting, and hiring decisions.

User profiles help with:

* Basic contact information
* Job posting identity
* Communication between users and technicians
* Review and feedback history

---

### Search and Nearby Discovery 🔎📍

Users can search by trade and browse nearby professionals.

This helps reduce:

* Time wasted searching manually
* Travel mismatch
* Delays in urgent repairs
* Difficulty finding suitable professionals

Examples of searchable trades include:

* Electricians
* Plumbers
* Masons
* Architects
* Interior designers
* Carpenters
* Painters
* Other construction technicians

### Job Posts 📝

Users can create posts describing their work requirements.

A job post can include:

* Work description
* Location
* Expected timeline
* Budget idea
* Images or project details
* Required trade or skill

Technicians can view relevant posts and respond if they are interested.

---

### Proposals / Bids 📑

Technicians can respond to job posts with structured proposals.

A proposal can include:

* 💰 Budget expectation or quotation guidance
* ⏱️ Timeline estimate
* 🛠️ Work approach
* 🧩 Related experience
* 📷 Previous project examples
* 📌 Scope assumptions

This helps users compare technicians clearly instead of choosing blindly or only selecting the cheapest option.

---

### Ratings and Reviews ⭐

After work is completed, users can leave feedback for technicians.

Reviews help future clients understand:

* Work quality
* Reliability
* Communication
* Professionalism
* Overall satisfaction

This creates long-term trust and helps good technicians stand out.

## Monetization Features 💼

LabourX includes multiple monetization features to support platform growth.

### Profile Boosts 🚀

Technicians can request profile boosts to increase their visibility.

A boosted profile may appear more prominently in the platform and can show badges such as:

* ⭐ Top
* ✓ Verified
* ✦ Featured

Boosting helps technicians attract more attention and win more work.

Admins can review boost requests from the admin panel and approve, reject, deactivate, or remove them.

---

### Post Boosts 📌

Job posts or related content can also be boosted to gain more visibility.

This is useful when a user wants faster responses from technicians or wants their job request to reach more professionals.

---

### Advertisements 📢

LabourX supports platform advertisements.

Advertisements can be used for:

* Promoting construction products
* Promoting service businesses
* Highlighting offers
* Showing sponsored content inside the platform

The admin dashboard tracks advertisement performance, including:

* 👁️ Total ad views
* 🖱️ Ad clicks
* 📊 Average CTR

CTR means Click-Through Rate. It shows how many users clicked an ad compared to how many viewed it.

---

### Donations / Buy Us a Coffee ☕

LabourX includes a donation feature where users can support the platform.

Users can:

* Choose a predefined donation amount
* Enter a custom amount
* Leave an optional message
* Complete a simulated card payment
* See a success confirmation

Admins can view donation revenue and recent donations from the dashboard.
